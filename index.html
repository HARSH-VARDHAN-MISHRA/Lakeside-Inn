About Us

Welcome to Lakeside Inn - Hotel on Mall Road Nainital

Welcome to Lakeside Inn, your premier destination nestled on the picturesque Mall Road of Nainital, Uttarakhand. With a legacy of hospitality spanning years, we pride ourselves on offering a serene retreat amidst the natural beauty of the Himalayas. Our charming inn is a perfect blend of modern comfort and timeless elegance, ensuring a memorable stay for every guest.

At Lakeside Inn, we believe in providing more than just accommodation; we offer an experience. Whether you're here for a leisurely vacation, a romantic getaway, or a corporate retreat, our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your every need is met with warmth and efficiency. From our cozy rooms boasting stunning views of the Naini Lake to our delectable dining options featuring local flavors and international cuisine, every aspect of your stay is carefully curated to exceed your expectations. Come, experience the tranquility and hospitality of Lakeside Inn, where every moment is crafted to perfection.
